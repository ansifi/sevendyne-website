Our Story

Founded in 2012, Sevendyne (brand: Sevendyne.com) began as a freelance platform providing app development services. In 2016, we registered as Sevendyne Consultancy Services LLP and expanded our services to comprehensive IT consultancy, serving clients across UK, Germany, Malaysia, Singapore, and beyond.

2012

Foundation

Launched Sevendyne as a freelance platform providing app development services. Started building a reputation for quality software development and client satisfaction.

2016

Company Registration

Registered as Sevendyne Consultancy Services LLP (brand: Sevendyne.com) with initial clients from the UK. Built a core team of around 10 people, establishing the foundation for scalable growth.

2019-Present

Global Expansion

Catered to multiple clients from Germany, Malaysia, and Singapore. Hired and managed 10+ resources across various projects, establishing ongoing partnerships in automotive, fintech, and technology sectors.

2024-2025

App Solutions + Five Service Tracks

Launched the Sevendyne App Solutions Hub for rapid product launches and formalised five specialised service tracks spanning full-stack engineering, AI, DevOps, CRM/payments, and C++ systems. 10+ enterprise engagements, supporting clients in 5+ countries.

Today, Sevendyne combines deep technical expertise with industry knowledge to deliver enterprise-grade solutions at competitive prices. Our journey from a freelance platform to a full-service IT consultancy reflects our commitment to growth, innovation, and client success.
