Frequently Asked Questions

What engagement models do you offer?

App Solutions projects follow milestone billing (40/40/20). Retainers are monthly with a 30-day exit, and enterprise programmes can be billed quarterly against performance KPIs.

Can we bundle multiple service tracks?

Yes. Many clients start with the App Solutions Hub and then add DevOps, AI, or other service retainers. We provide bundled commercials and a dedicated engagement manager.

Do you work with international billing?

We invoice in INR by default, but USD/EUR billing is available via Stripe, Wise, and international wire transfers. GST is waived for eligible export services.

How quickly can we start?

Launch Sprints can begin within 5 working days. Retainers typically kick off within 2-3 weeks once scope, team allocation, and contracts are finalised.
